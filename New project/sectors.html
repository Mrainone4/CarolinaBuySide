Sectors

Sector coverage is a core part of the Carolina BuySide experience.

Members build deeper industry familiarity through sector-focused work, recurring idea generation, and stock pitches designed to sharpen research discipline and communication for buy-side work across private equity (PE), growth equity (GE), and venture capital (VC).

Research businesses, follow industries, and present investment views.

Carolina BuySide uses sector teams to create a more structured approach to investment work. Members follow industries over time, compare businesses, discuss current themes, and translate their research into stock pitches and broader investing conversations relevant to PE, GE, and VC decision-making.

Pitch work gives members repeated practice building a thesis, defending assumptions, and communicating an investment view clearly in front of peers.
