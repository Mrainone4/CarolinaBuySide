CAROLINA BUYSIDE

A More Formal Home for Carolina's Student BuySide Community.

Carolina BuySide is a student-led organization at UNC-Chapel Hill built around rigorous investing education, professional development, and preparation for careers across buy-side paths. We explicitly cover private equity (PE), growth equity (GE), and venture capital (VC), with members building conviction through research, stock pitches, sector work, and collaborative discussion.

Market Context

Built in the spirit of professional investing institutions.

Carolina BuySide mirrors a more institutional approach to investing work through sector coverage, stock pitches, and disciplined research discussion in a collaborative student setting.

Full BuySide Coverage

The club is intentionally broad across PE, GE, and VC so members build a complete view of private-market opportunities and decision frameworks.

Research Discipline

Members are trained to frame a thesis, test assumptions, and defend conclusions with evidence rather than surface-level market narratives.

Pitch Communication

Structured pitch sessions develop the ability to communicate clearly under pressure, receive feedback, and iterate quickly.

Long-Term Development

A semester-to-semester model helps members compound judgment over time through repeated sector work, collaboration, and practical execution.
